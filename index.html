BLOCK FRIDAY

initiated by Holstee

Let's take back Black Friday and indulge in the people around us. It's a perfect time to be mindful of our time and spending, so what are you Blocking Friday for this holiday season?
how are we blocking friday?
how can you join us?
Block Friday is all about taking the rare chance to spend time with your loved ones and really enjoying the holidays in the most mindful way we know how. We just want you to smile and, well, not tear your hair out (or have someone do it for you).

RECORD YOURSELF We want to know what you're Blocking Friday for! Record a video and tag it #blockfriday on YouTube sharing that with us (and we'll add it to the video series above!). Simple.

TWEET! Broadcast your intention to be awesome to the world. Try something like this: "This year, I'm Blocking Off Friday to throw a potluck with my friends from high school. #blockfriday"

HOOK YOUR FRIENDS Go out and ask five friends what they'll be doing on Black Friday. Propose some sweet alternatives or come up with something awesome together. Then tweet it. Obviously. #blockfriday.
