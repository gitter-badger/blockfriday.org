Initiated by Holstee.

  • why us + why you
  • what you could do

    • We're looking for companies and organizations with a social conscience to do three things:

    LAYOVER On Nov. 23, insert this layover on your home page. People will click through the layover before proceeding to your site. It’s a way to reiterate our shared values around mindful consumption.

    EMAIL The week of November 23, send a note to your community. Remind them to consider their impact up and down the chain of consumption as they do their holiday shopping.

    TWEET Reach out to your community. Remember that companies have families too, so feel free to single out customers, partners and friends of your brand to tell them how you feel about them. Tell them why you’re participating in Block Friday. And don’t forget the hashtag — #blockfriday.

    That's it. Of course, you can go above and beyond, and we're more than happy to talk about what that might entail, but these are the basics.
